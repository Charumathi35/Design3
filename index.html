WELCOME TO

Established in 1975, Pricol Holdings is a leading business conglomerate Headquartered in Coimbatore, India. Pricol commenced its operations in the field of Automotive Instruments and today is a leading player in the fields of Automotive Components & Solutions, Engineering, Hospitality, Corporate & Leisure Travel, Cargo, and Realty. Today, the group employs more than 5500 professional workforce across the globe with a turnover of over INR 3000 Crore and a list of award winning brands under its umbrella.
